CropSmart

CropSmart, our prototype app, specializes in detecting diseases affecting plant images. Through cutting-edge image recognition technology, CropSmart swiftly identifies signs of diseases with remarkable accuracy. By harnessing the power of machine learning algorithms, our app continuously refines its detection capabilities, ensuring precise diagnosis of plant ailments. Upon detection, CropSmart goes beyond mere identification, providing tailored recommendations for effective disease management. With CropSmart, farmers can confidently address plant health issues, ensuring optimal yields and safeguarding crop productivity.
